About Me

A Lead UX/UI & Web Dev based in Cambodia

I design and develop services for customers of all sizes, specializing in creating stylish, modern websites, web services and online stores. My passion is to design digital user experiences through the bold interface and meaningful interactions.

I am a creative and passionate web developer with over 5 years of experience in designing and building responsive websites and applications. I have a strong knowledge of HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and PHP, as well as experience with SEO, UX and UI design. I enjoy working on challenging projects that require problem-solving and innovation. I am always eager to learn new skills and technologies to enhance my web development expertise. I am also a team player who can collaborate effectively with other developers, designers and clients.
